Datsun go+

Datsun India might not have received the best of response for the GO hatchback, especially after the Global NCAP crash test results, but that has not deterred the company from trying to redeem itself; they decided to go after the MPV segment, and consequently created a new segment with the sub-4 metre MPV GO+.

The front profile of the car is similar to that of the GO hatchback; in fact, the side profile too remains similar to the hatchback till about the C-pillar.

Though Datsun managed to add a third row, it is meant only for kids. Also Read: Datsun Go+ MPV Review Powering the vehicle is the same, 1.2-litre petrol engine that does duty on the GO hatchback.

This engine churns out a maximum power output of 67bhp and peak torque of 104Nm.

Transmitting power to the front wheels of the car is a 5-speed manual gearbox.

The engine is claimed to return an impressive fuel economy of 20.62Km/l.
