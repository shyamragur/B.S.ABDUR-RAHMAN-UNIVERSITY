Hyundai Eon

When it first arrived in the market in 2011, the Eon was deemed to be the first real competitor for the Maruti Suzuki Alto, and it was indeed.

Some of you may be inclined to remind us of the Chevrolet Spark, but the truth is it failed to give Alto any competition at all.

Though initially the Eon was underpowered, but Hyundai fitted it with the 1-litre, 3 cylinder engine from its updated Kappa II family of engines last year.

The car also looks good, thanks to the Fluidic design language it adopts.

Also Read: Hyundai Eon Review It is still available in the 814cc avatar, but the 1-litre engine comes with latest technologies like Dual VTVT, DOHC and lightweight cylinder block, and punches out 68bbhp while the mileage stands at 20.3Km/l. Hyundai also gives the customers an option of buying the Eon in the LPG avatar.
